Palestine 2025
Under Siege

Continuous Assault

Air-strikes, artillery shelling, demolition campaigns and rampaging settler violence have not stopped. Cease-fires collapse within days and every lull is followed by deadlier waves that flatten entire city blocks and bury families under the rubble of their own homes.

Humanitarian Collapse

Food, clean water, fuel and medical supplies are chronically short. Famine-like conditions have been documented by multiple UN agencies. Hospitals operate without electricity or basic medicines, forcing doctors to perform surgery by torchlight and leaving premature babies to die in incubators.

West-Bank Settler Violence

2025 is already the deadliest year for West-Bank Palestinians since the Second Intifada. Settlers, often escorted by soldiers, have torched entire villages, shot live rounds at unarmed farmers and uprooted ancient olive groves that families have tended for centuries.

Diplomatic Shifts

In 2025 the UK, Australia, Canada and Spain formally recognised the State of Palestine, adding new political pressure even as daily death tolls continue to climb and families remain buried under rubble.
