Sudan 2025
Catastrophic War

El-Fasher Falls

After a prolonged siege the Rapid Support Forces seized El-Fasher, the last major SAF stronghold in North Darfur. Civilians were caught in cross-fire and thousands fled eastward under artillery fire and without water or shelter.

Famine Confirmed by UN

On 1 November 2025 the Integrated Food-Security Phase Classification (IPC) officially classified Zamzam camp and parts of Kordofan as IPC 5 (Famine). At least 755,000 people are now facing starvation and aid trucks are still being looted at checkpoints.

Displacement Crisis

More than 8 million Sudanese are internally displaced; another 2 million have fled to Chad, South Sudan and Egypt. The world’s largest displacement crisis is unfolding with minimal media attention and only 38% of the UN appeal funded.
